## About Parti

Parti is a digital agora for civic dialogue and action. Over the past decade, we've been building digital public squares where citizens gather, dialogue, act, and decide together. This document records the 100 features and programs that have grown from that decade of practice — what we've already built, what we're building now, and what we believe should come next.

## A Living Document

This is not a static catalog. It is a living document. New features are added as Parti builds them, and suggestions for better descriptions are reviewed and incorporated. This list is not a conclusion — it is a beginning.

Corrections, additions, and suggestions are welcome through [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/parti-coop/digital-democracy-100/issues/new) or Pull Requests.
